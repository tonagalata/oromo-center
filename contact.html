Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Do you provide services in Afaan Oromo?

Yes! We have staff members who speak Afaan Oromo and can provide services in your preferred language.

Are your services free?

Most of our services are free or low-cost. We work with government agencies and nonprofits to provide affordable assistance.

Can I get help if I'm not in one of your main cities?

Yes! We provide virtual assistance and can connect you with local resources in your area.

How can I volunteer with the Oromo Center?

We welcome volunteers! Please contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities in your area.

Do you offer cultural events and programs?

Absolutely! We host regular cultural events, language classes, and community gatherings. Check our events calendar for upcoming activities.

Ready to get started? We're here to help you succeed.
